# 🍽️ ZeroWaste AI – Smart Food Redistribution System

> Turning surplus food into timely meals using AI 🚀

## 📌 Overview
ZeroWaste AI is an AI-powered platform that predicts surplus food generation and connects it with nearby NGOs and shelters in real time. The goal is to reduce food waste while helping feed people in need.

## 🚨 Problem
Large amounts of edible food are wasted daily in restaurants, hostels, and events, while many people still suffer from hunger. The lack of a real-time system to identify and redistribute surplus food leads to this gap.

## 💡 Solution
ZeroWaste AI uses machine learning to:
- Predict surplus food before it goes to waste  
- Connect food donors with nearby NGOs  
- Enable quick and efficient redistribution  

## 🤖 AI Approach
The system uses machine learning models to predict food surplus based on:
- Day of the week  
- Meal type  
- Prepared vs consumed quantity  
- Weather conditions  
- Event presence  

This helps optimize redistribution and reduce wastage.

## ⚙️ Features
- 🏪 Donor Portal (input food details)  
- 🤖 AI-based surplus prediction  
- 🤝 NGO Matching System  
- 📊 Impact Dashboard (meals saved, waste reduced)  
- ⏱️ Freshness alert for urgent pickups
